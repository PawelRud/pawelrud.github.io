Biography

I am a third year PhD student working within the probability group Prob-L@b at University of Bath. I am mainly interested in random walks in random environments. My current research focuses on reinforced random walks and is supervised by Daniel Kious. I completed my MMath in Mathematics at Durham University 2016 - 2020. My dissertation was on Percolation: Methods for obtaining the upper and lower bounds for critical probabilities supervised by Mikhail Menshikov.
