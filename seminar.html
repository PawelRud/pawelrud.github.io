Meet the organisers

The Postgraduate Online Probability Seminar (POPS) is an online platform for doctoral students and post-docs from all over the world to present and promote their research to a global audience in areas related to probability and its applications.

The seminar takes place on the last Wednesday of every month at (TBC) on (Zoom/Teams), so make sure to join the mailing list to receive reminders and stay informed about upcoming events!

If you would like to give a talk, please contact one of the organisers.
